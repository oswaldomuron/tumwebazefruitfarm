Tumwebaze Fruitfarm

Tumwebaze Fruitfarm is a family-owned, small-scale integrated agricultural farm established in 2004. The farm spans 3.7 acres of fertile land and is primarily focused on fruit production. Over the years, we have specialized in growing a variety of delicious fruits, including:

  • Mangoes
  • Guavas
  • Paw paws
  • Grapes
  • Passion fruits
  • Apples

Our goal at Tumwebaze Fruitfarm is to provide fresh, high-quality fruits while maintaining sustainable farming practices that benefit both our community and the environment.
